Description:
  • Pugs are available in three colors: all-black, silver, or apricot-fawn with a black facial mask.

  • Pugs have a wide range of human-like expressions—surprise, happiness, and curiosity.—

  • Has charmed owners for generations thanks to their huge, round heads, enormous, sparkling eyes, and wrinkled face.
  • The Shih Tzu coat is available in a variety of hues, including black, white, gold, etc.

  • Few dogs can match the beauty of a well-groomed Shih Tzu. This vivacious charmer lives her life being adorable.

  • The Shih Tzu is renowned for its exceptional compassion for kids.
  • Samoyeds have thick, all-white coats. They are social, cunning, and intelligent dogs who demand affection.

  • Samoyeds are large but graceful dogs that can range in height at the shoulder from 19 to just over 23 inches.
  • Chows are large, powerful canines that can reach a shoulder height of 20 inches.

  • Red, black, blue, cinnamon, or cream-colored coats in various textures are common on chows.

  • They are distant from strangers but devoted to their loved ones no matter what.
  • The medium-sized, well-built Golden Retriever is known for its glossy, glistening golden coat, which gives the breed its name.

  • A breed characteristic is the dog's straight nose, short ears, and friendly, intelligent eyes.

  • Golden retrievers make friendly, dependable, eager-to-please family dogs who are also rather simple to train.
